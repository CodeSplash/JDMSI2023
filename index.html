The third Edition of

The Doctoral Days on Mathematics and Engineering Sciences

13-14 March 2023, ENSA Khouribga, Morocco

About the conference

Our aim and objectives :

The Doctoral days of Mathematics and Engineering Sciences event ("Journée doctorale en Mathématiques et Science d'Ingénieur - JDMSI'2023") is a unique opportunity for PhD students to present their innovative research work and discuss it with a community of specialists in the field. This event brings together professors, scientists, researchers and professionals from the academic community to explore the latest advances in mathematics and engineering science. Doctoral students will have the opportunity to present their research in detail to an attentive audience, to obtain constructive feedback and to meet potential colleagues and mentors. This day also provides an opportunity to discover new ideas and approaches in the fields of mathematics and engineering science, which can stimulate future collaboration and cooperation. In short, it is a stimulating platform for doctoral students to share their passion for research and enrich their academic career.

Topics

Topics of interest to our event include but are not limited to:

Applied Mathematics and Optimisation
Image Processing and Computer Vision
Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence
Data Mining and Analytics
Computational Methods and Algorithms
Computer and Data Science Applications
