About the conference

Our aim and objectives :

The Laboratory of Process Engineering, Computer Science and Mathematics (LIPIM) organizes the third edition of the Doctoral days of Mathematics and Engineering Sciences event ("Journée doctorale en Mathématiques et Science d'Ingénieur - JDMSI'2023"). JDMSI'2023 is a unique opportunity for PhD students to present their innovative research work and discuss it with a community of specialists in the field. This event brings together professors, scientists, researchers and professionals from the academic community to explore the latest advances in mathematics and engineering science. Doctoral students will have the opportunity to present their research in detail to an attentive audience, to obtain constructive feedback and to meet potential colleagues and mentors. This day also provides an opportunity to discover new ideas and approaches in the fields of mathematics and engineering science, which can stimulate future collaboration and cooperation. In short, it is a stimulating platform for doctoral students to share their passion for research and enrich their academic career.

Call For Abstracts

Please register and submit your abstract following the guidelines below:

Prospective authors are kindly encouraged to contribute to and help shape the doctoral days through submissions of their research abstracts, extended abstracts. Submissions should contain original results obtained by the participant which demonstrates his recent advances in any topic related to the fields of interest.

The abstracts must be in English and the number of pages should not exceed 2 pages. We urge our participants to include a concise and clear description of their works in the form of a clear title, an abstract motivating the topic and describing the results as well as references and keywords.

All abstracts will be reviewed by the committee based on their originality, significance, methodological soundness, and clarity of exposition. Submissions of Abstract should be made before Monday March 6, 2023 to be considered for communication in this event.
